üêß 1. The Basics

Welcome to Low Hanging Fruit! You control a hungry penguin on an endless adventure. Your goal is simple: collect fruit, avoid bombs, and don't starve!

Controls

Tap to jump and collect items from trees. Swipe up while jumping for a higher jump to reach special items like Mana Bananas, the Wizard, or the Leprechaun that appear above the trees.

The Hunger Bar

Your penguin has a hunger bar that constantly depletes. If it empties completely, your penguin starves and the game ends!

üí° Tip: Collect fruit regularly to keep your hunger bar filled. Don't get too greedy chasing multipliers if your hunger is low!
‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: As you progress, your penguin moves faster, meaning you pass trees quicker and your hunger depletes faster relative to opportunities. Stay alert in the late game!

‚ö° 3. Multipliers

This is the key to high scores! Understanding multipliers is essential for competing on the leaderboards.

Two Types of Multipliers

üî• Streak Multiplier

Builds automatically as you collect items without missing. Resets if you miss a tree or take damage.

‚ö° Pickup Multiplier

Found in mystery boxes. Temporarily boosts your score multiplier.

Stacking for Maximum Points

Both multiplier types can stack together. Combining them is the real secret to maximizing your fruit count and climbing the leaderboards!

üí° Strategy: Build your streak first, then grab pickup multipliers from mystery boxes. The combination is where the real points come from!
